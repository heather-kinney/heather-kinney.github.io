Biography

I am a Coastal Restoration Scientist at The Nature Conservancy, working on marine and coastal ecology in Rhode Island. I received my M.A. in Marine Affairs from the University of Rhode Island and my B.S. in Marine Biology from Roger Williams University.

Education: M.A. in Marine Affairs, University of Rhode Island, 2017 | B.S. in Marine Biology, Roger Williams University, 2014

Current Position: Coastal Restoration Scientist at The Nature Conservancy (08/2022 - Present)

Previous Experience: Coastal Restoration Technician, Seasonal Grow-Out Technician, Research Assistant, and Water Quality Tester

  • Member, Rhode Island Coastal Restoration Advisory Committee
  • Advisory Panelist, Northeast Fish Habitat Partnership
  • Speaker, Marine Science Outreach Programs

When I’m not working on coastal restoration, you’ll find me hiking along Rhode Island’s shorelines, kayaking, or exploring new seafood recipes in my kitchen. I also enjoy volunteering with community cleanups and advocating for sustainable fisheries.
