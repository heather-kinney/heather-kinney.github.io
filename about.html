Biography

I am a Coastal Restoration Scientist at The Nature Conservancy. I received my M.A. in Marine Affarirs from The University of Rhode Island and my B.S. in Marine Biology from Roger Williams University. My work focuses on marine and coastal ecology in Rhode Island.

My research focuses on marine and coastal ecology and intersections with human uses. I am currently working on projects related to river herring passage, fish habitat enhancement, marine debris interception, and stormwater monitoring.
