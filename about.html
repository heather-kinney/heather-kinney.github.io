Biography

I am a Coastal Restoration Scientist at The Nature Conservancy. I received my M.A. in Marine Affairs from The University of Rhode Island and my B.S. in Marine Biology from Roger Williams University. My work focuses on marine and coastal ecology in Rhode Island.

Beyond Science

When I’m not working on coastal restoration, you’ll find me hiking along Rhode Island’s shorelines, kayaking, or exploring new seafood recipes in my kitchen. I also enjoy volunteering with community cleanups and advocating for sustainable fisheries.
