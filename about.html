Biography

I am a Coastal Restoration Scientist at The Nature Conservancy. I received my M.A. in Marine Affairs from The University of Rhode Island and my B.S. in Marine Biology from Roger Williams University. My work focuses on marine and coastal ecology in Rhode Island.
