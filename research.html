Current Projects

Juvenile fish seine survey is conducted at 12 stations in the upper bay from May through October. Applies the same sampling methodology and gear as TNC Great Salt Pond (Diandra Hall) and DMF coastal ponds (Katie Rodrigue). Applies the same sampling methodology and different gear as DMF Bay-wide survey (Anna Gerber-Williams). This survey work began in 2016. Completed annual survey data is available from 2017-2024. Site photos were incorporated in 2024 to track surrounding habitat change over time.
